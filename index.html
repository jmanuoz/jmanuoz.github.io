About

Juan Manuel Ortiz de Zarate is a highly accomplished computer scientist and data scientist with over a decade of experience in software development and more than five years of experience in data science. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science and is currently finishing his PhD in Artificial Intelligence.

Throughout his career, Juan has demonstrated a deep understanding of the latest technologies and trends in the field of computer science. He has worked on a wide range of projects, from developing complex software applications to designing and implementing cutting-edge data analytics solutions.

In addition to his work as a data scientist, Juan has also been a professor at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Buenos Aires, where he taught the Data Science subject. He has been recognized for his exceptional teaching skills and his ability to inspire and motivate students to achieve their full potential.

Since 2019, Juan has been working as a data scientist at Toptal, a leading global network of top freelance software developers, designers, and finance experts. In this role, he has been responsible for developing and implementing data-driven solutions that help clients achieve their business goals.

Juan is a highly motivated and dedicated professional who is committed to delivering exceptional results for his clients and colleagues. He is passionate about using his expertise in computer science and data science to make a positive impact on the world and is always looking for new and innovative ways to solve complex problems.
